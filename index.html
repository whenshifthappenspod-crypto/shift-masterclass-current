Frequently Asked Questions

How is SHIFT different from other crypto courses?

SHIFT is taught by industry practitioners—founders, investors, and experts managing billions in crypto—not influencers selling courses. We focus on long-term wealth building, risk management, and fundamentals, not moonshot gambling. Plus, you get an internationally recognized certificate to validate your expertise.

Who teaches the masterclass?

Instructors include guests from the When Shift Happens podcast: founders of major crypto companies, institutional investors, DeFi protocol creators, and industry veterans with real-world experience building and managing billions in digital assets.

What's the time commitment?

The program runs for 12 weeks with one 90-minute live session per week. Sessions are scheduled for professional availability (evenings/weekends). Expect 2-3 hours per week for the live session plus self-study.

Are sessions live or pre-recorded?

Live and interactive. We believe in real-time learning with direct Q&A access to instructors. Sessions are recorded for review, but live attendance is strongly encouraged for maximum value.

Do I need prior crypto experience?

No prior experience required. The curriculum starts with Bitcoin fundamentals and progressively builds to advanced topics. The program is designed for curious professionals who are new to crypto but serious about learning properly.

What's included in the certificate?

Upon completion, you receive an internationally recognized certificate validated by When Shift Happens and program sponsors. Perfect for LinkedIn, your CV, or demonstrating Web3 expertise to colleagues and investors.

How much does it cost?

Investment details are shared upon application approval. This is executive-level education comparable to MBA programs—premium pricing reflects the quality, expert access, and career value. Think INSEAD or Wharton for Web3.

Is there a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes. If after the first two modules you feel SHIFT isn't delivering practitioner-level value, we offer a full refund. We're confident in the quality and want committed participants who benefit fully.
